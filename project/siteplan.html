Budgeting Tools for Beginners

Site Purpose

Budgeting is an important skill that is often not well practiced for many reasons. I am not good at setting and sticking to a budget. There are many reasons I fail to keep or set a budget that I feel many people share:

  • Living Paycheck to Paycheck
    • It can difficult to start to a budget when you feel like you have nothing to work with.
  • Don't Know Where to Start
    • Budgeting isn't always taught in school or by parents, so it many people don't know how to get started.
  • Don't Think a Budget is Important or Will Help
    • It's easy to think that you're already living frugally, or that your on top of your bills so a budget won't help you. This in part comes from the idea that a budget is just a way to save money. While that is one reason people create and follow a budget, budgets are a great tool to manage your money.
I wanted to put together resources about budgeting, specifically some educational material and some calculators to help people understand how to budget and give them a place to get started.

Scenarios

I expect that people with the following questions will find this site helpful:

  • How do I start budgeting?
  • How can a budget save me money?
  • Where is my money going?
